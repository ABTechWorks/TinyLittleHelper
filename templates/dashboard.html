Device Monitoring – How It Works

Quick Notes:
  • Devices are monitored using the TinyLittleHelper app — not IP pings.
  • To add a device, you must log into your account and download the helper.
  • Install and run the helper on the exact device you want to monitor.
  • Once running, the helper automatically registers the device and keeps it online in the background.
  • After installing the helper, refresh this page to see the device appear.
